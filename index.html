about arcade shooters

Built with pure HTML5 Canvas — no plugins, no installs. Just open and play on any device.

Mobile-First Controls
Drag your cannon with touch or mouse. Arrow buttons for precision. Responsive to any screen size.

Dynamic Audio
Every shot, squish, and level-up has its own satisfying sound — all generated in real-time via Web Audio API. Zero audio files needed.
