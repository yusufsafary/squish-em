Guide

How to Play SQUISH 'EM!

Master the basics in 60 seconds. Become a blob-squishing legend in an hour.

🎯 The Objective

1
Blobs fall from the top
Colourful blob enemies spawn at the top of the screen and slowly drift downward. They each have different speeds, HP, and behaviours.
2
Shoot them before they reach the bottom
Your cannon auto-fires every 0.5 seconds. Move it left and right so bullets hit blobs. If a blob reaches the bottom, you lose a life ❤️.
3
Survive and score as high as possible
You start with 3 lives. Collect power-ups, chain combos, and defeat bosses to rack up points. The game ends when you run out of lives.
💡 Your cannon fires automatically — you just need to focus on moving into position. No button mashing required.

🕹️ Controls

👆
Touch Drag
Touch and drag anywhere on the rail or cannon to move it left and right. Works on any touchscreen.
🖱️
Mouse Drag
Click and drag the cannon or rail with your mouse. Works on desktop and laptop.
⌨️
Arrow Keys
Use ← → arrow keys on keyboard to slide the cannon. Also works while dragging.
◀▶
On-Screen Buttons
Tap the ◀ ▶ arrow buttons shown at the bottom of the game screen.
⏸
Pause
Press P or Escape on keyboard to pause and resume the game.

🔥 Combo System

Kill multiple blobs within 1.5 seconds of each other to trigger a combo multiplier. The counter resets if you go 1.5s without a kill.

ChainMultiplierExample (Green Blob)
1 kill×110 pts
2 kills×1.515 pts per kill
3 kills×220 pts per kill
4+ kills×330 pts per kill
💡 Stacking a ×3 combo with the Score Multiplier power-up gives you a ×6 multiplier. Golden Blob during that = 600 points!

⬆️ Progression

L
Level Up every 15 kills
Each new level unlocks faster and tougher blob types, and increases the maximum number of blobs on screen simultaneously.
S
Speed scales every 10 kills
Every 10 kills, all blobs get 10% faster. Speed is capped at 300% of base — but by that point, things are truly hectic.
B
Boss at levels 5, 10, 15…
A massive Boss Blob with 20 HP appears. Its speed accelerates as you chip away its health. It shoots projectiles every ~2.5 seconds — dodge or use your Shield!

⚡ Power-up Tips

🔥
Rapid Fire + Combo
Activate Rapid Fire then chain as many kills as possible in 1.5s to maximise your combo multiplier.
✦✦✦
Triple Shot for Crowds
When many blobs cluster, Triple Shot clears columns efficiently. Also excellent for hitting zigzagging Golden Blobs.
🛡
Save Shield for Boss
The Shield absorbs one boss projectile or one blob that reaches the bottom — ideal insurance during a boss fight.
×2
Score Mult + Boss Kill
Try to have Score Multiplier active when you land the killing blow on a Boss. 500 pts becomes 1000 pts instantly.

🏆 Pro Tips

★
Prioritise by threat, not proximity
Red blobs are fast — they reach the bottom quickly even if spawned late. Target them before the slow Blue blob that's been floating for a while.
★
Centre position = maximum coverage
Staying near the centre lets you reach any blob quickly. Only move far to the edges when a blob is about to escape at that side.
★
Hunt Golden Blobs immediately
A Golden Blob is worth 10 Green Blobs. They zigzag but have 1 HP — a single bullet kills them. Always divert to intercept them.
★
During a boss fight — keep moving
Boss projectiles travel at a fixed trajectory. Moving constantly means they're less likely to align with your cannon's position.
