Guide

How to Play SQUISH 'EM!

Master the basics in 60 seconds. Become a blob-squishing legend in an hour.

How It Works

Built for the browser,
not ported to it

Designed mobile-first from day one. All rendering, physics, and audio run client-side with zero external libs.

🎮
Touch and Mouse Controls
Drag your cannon with a finger or mouse. On-screen arrow buttons for precision. Works on any screen size without layout shifts.
🔊
Synthesised Audio
Every shot, squish, and level fanfare is generated at runtime via Web Audio API oscillators. No audio files, no network requests for sound.
⚡
60 FPS Rendering
Object pooling and delta-time capping keep the loop stable at 60fps on mid-range phones. requestAnimationFrame throughout.
🏆
Persistent High Score
Your best run is saved to localStorage and shown on the title screen with a golden trophy celebration on each new record.
💥
Combo Multipliers
Chain kills within 1.5 seconds for up to 3x point multipliers. Stack with Score Multiplier for six-figure runs.
👾
Boss Battles
Every 5 levels a Boss Blob spawns with a live health bar, rage face, speed escalation, and homing projectiles every 2.5 seconds.

The Objective

1
Blobs fall from the top
Colourful blob enemies spawn at the top of the screen and drift downward. Each type has different speed, HP, and movement behaviour.
2
Shoot them before they reach the bottom
Your cannon auto-fires every 0.5 seconds. Move it left and right so bullets hit blobs. If a blob reaches the bottom, you lose a life.
3
Survive and score as high as possible
You start with 3 lives. Collect power-ups, chain combos, and defeat bosses to rack up points. The game ends when you run out of lives.
💡 Your cannon fires automatically — you just need to focus on moving into position. No button mashing required.

Controls

👆
Touch Drag
Touch and drag anywhere on the rail or cannon to move it. Works on any touchscreen.
🖱️
Mouse Drag
Click and drag the cannon or rail with your mouse. Works on desktop and laptop.
⌨️
Arrow Keys
Use left and right arrow keys on keyboard to slide the cannon. Works while dragging too.
◀▶
On-Screen Buttons
Tap the arrow buttons at the bottom of the game screen for thumb-friendly control.
⏸
Pause
Press P or Escape on keyboard to pause and resume the game at any time.

Combo System

Kill multiple blobs within 1.5 seconds of each other to trigger a combo multiplier. The counter resets if you go 1.5s without a kill.

ChainMultiplierExample (Green Blob)
1 killx110 pts
2 killsx1.515 pts per kill
3 killsx220 pts per kill
4+ killsx330 pts per kill
💡 Stacking a x3 combo with the Score Multiplier power-up gives you a x6 multiplier. A Golden Blob during that window = 600 points.

Progression

L
Level up every 15 kills
Each new level unlocks faster and tougher blob types, and increases the maximum number of blobs on screen simultaneously.
S
Speed scales every 10 kills
Every 10 kills, all blobs get 10% faster. Speed is capped at 300% base — but by that point, things are genuinely hectic.
B
Boss at levels 5, 10, 15...
A massive Boss Blob with 20 HP appears. Its speed increases as you chip away its health, and it fires projectiles every 2.5 seconds.

Power-up Tips

🔥
Rapid Fire + Combo
Activate Rapid Fire then chain as many kills as possible within 1.5s to build your combo multiplier fast.
✦✦✦
Triple Shot for Crowds
When blobs cluster, Triple Shot clears entire columns. Also excellent for catching zigzagging Golden Blobs.
🛡
Save Shield for Boss
The Shield absorbs one boss projectile or one blob that reaches the bottom — ideal insurance during a boss fight.
x2
Score Mult + Boss Kill
Try to have Score Multiplier active when you land the killing blow on a Boss. 500 pts becomes 1000 pts instantly.

Pro Tips

★
Prioritise by threat, not proximity
Red blobs are fast — they reach the bottom quickly even when spawned late. Target them before a slow Blue blob that has been floating for a while.
★
Centre position covers the most ground
Staying near the centre lets you reach any blob quickly. Only move to the edges when a blob is about to escape at that side.
★
Hunt Golden Blobs immediately
A Golden Blob is worth 10 Green Blobs and has only 1 HP. Always divert to intercept it — a single bullet kills it.
★
Keep moving during boss fights
Boss projectiles travel in fixed trajectories. Moving constantly means they are less likely to align with your cannon's position.
